Khalid ibn al-Walid

592AD: Born in the Quraysh tribe of Mecca, in what is now Saudi Arabia.
622AD: Converted to Islam after teachings of the Prophet Muhammad 'PBOH'
624AD: Participated in the Battle of Badr a significant early battle in Islamic history
632AD: Continued Militiary campaigns and leadership after the dead of the Prophet Muhammad 'PBOH'
638AD: End of his leadershipof the Armies and died at the age of 60
